# Social Media Avatar Prompt Example

## Scenario

Generate a stylized but identity-consistent avatar for social media.

## Input

- Portrait reference
- Style preference (clean, cute, minimal, stylized)

## Prompt template

Use the provided portrait as the base reference. Preserve the original identity and facial structure. Create a clean and expressive social media avatar with simplified background and controlled stylization.

Apply a soft stylized treatment (illustration-like or semi-realistic), but keep the face recognizable. Use minimal composition, centered framing, and subtle lighting. Background should be simple (solid color, soft gradient, or light texture).

Optional elements: small icons, subtle glow, soft shadow.

## Constraints

- Avoid over-stylization
- Avoid distortion of face proportions
- Avoid cluttered background
- Keep composition centered and clean

## Output

Square image suitable for profile use.

## Review checklist

- Is the avatar recognizable as the original person?
- Is the composition clean and readable at small size?
- Is the style consistent and not excessive?
