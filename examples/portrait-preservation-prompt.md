# Portrait Preservation Prompt Example

## Scenario

Generate an image while preserving the identity of a real person.

## Prompt template

Use the provided portrait as the base reference. Keep original face identity, do not alter facial structure, preserve face angle, hairstyle, expression, and proportions. Avoid generating a generic or beautified face. The output should resemble the real person as closely as possible.

Apply a controlled visual style (e.g., cinematic, soft light, minimal watercolor), but ensure that the style does not override identity. The face should remain realistic and recognizable.

## Constraints

- Do not change facial proportions
- Do not over-smooth skin
- Avoid plastic or artificial texture
- Avoid exaggerated anime or illustration transformation

## Use case

This prompt is used when identity accuracy is more important than style, such as graduation posters, commemorative portraits, or profile images.

## Review checklist

- Is the person still recognizable?
- Is the face structure unchanged?
- Is the style applied subtly rather than dominating the face?
